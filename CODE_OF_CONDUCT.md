# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

Contributors must communicate respectfully, review ideas rather than identities, welcome good-faith questions, and keep technical disagreement evidence-based. Harassment, discrimination, threats, doxxing, sexualized conduct, sustained disruption, and disclosure of another person's private information are unacceptable.

## Project spaces

This standard applies to issues, pull requests, reviews, discussions, demonstrations, and other spaces where someone represents the project.

## Enforcement

Report conduct concerns privately to the repository owner or organization administrator through the hosting platform's private reporting channel. Do not place sensitive reports in public issues. Maintainers may edit or remove content, warn participants, restrict participation, or ban accounts according to severity and recurrence.

Enforcement decisions should protect reporters' privacy, document the relevant behavior, and avoid retaliation. The project owner must publish a dedicated conduct contact before opening broad public contribution.
